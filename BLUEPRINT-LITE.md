# Advocacia Premium - Blueprint Lite

## Positioning

Premium legal website with editorial restraint, institutional clarity, and a structure designed to increase trust for consultive legal services.

## Core Direction

- dark and refined palette with sober accents;
- clear hierarchy to communicate expertise and authority;
- institutional composition without feeling generic;
- strong balance between branding and conversion.

## Main Sections

1. Hero
2. About / positioning
3. Practice areas
4. Social proof or credibility
5. Legal content
6. Contact
7. Footer

## Interaction Logic

- the first screen should establish authority immediately;
- practice areas and support content must reduce perceived risk;
- the overall rhythm should feel premium, not promotional;
- the structure should remain easy to adapt to different specialties.

## Design Tokens

- Base background: charcoal or deep neutral
- Main text: ivory or off-white
- Accent: restrained metallic or burgundy tone
- Support text: muted neutral

## Why This Works

- creates trust for a high-friction service category;
- feels institutional without becoming cold or generic;
- adapts well to boutique, corporate, or specialist legal operations.
